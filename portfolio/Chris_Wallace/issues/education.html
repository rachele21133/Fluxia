Education

Education is near and dear to my heart. I come from a family of teachers and experienced the value of a strong public education system first-hand. The education I received at Jonathan Alder High School enabled me to succeed in college at The Ohio State University and earn a law degree afterwards.

I want to empower teachers to provide students with a world-class education that prepares them to enter the workforce or pursue a college degree. I believe that local school boards should have a strong voice in developing education policy, as that is where parents can best make decisions for their children.
