About

FLUXIA is an Ohio based company founded in 2016 by web designer and mobile application developer, Rachel Havas.

Rachel Havas has been a computer programmer with a focus on web design and application development since 2012. She is fluent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. While pursuing her Computer Science and Engineering degree at The Ohio State University, she created mobile apps using Java and PHP, and is proficient in C+, C++, and Python. Rachel loves using her creativity and technical skills to help make businesses, blogs, and other big ideas come to life. Her work maintains a minimalist approach that strives to give the user an easy and intuitive experience as they explore your content.

Rachel is a resident of Columbus, Ohio, where she lives with her husband Andrew, and their dog, Selene.
